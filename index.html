Meet Principal Brown

Principal Nigel Brown has been the principal of Elmore Junior High since 2005.

He graduated with honors from the Brain Academy.
His principal's license is proudly displayed in his office with the official Dole Chiquita Banana seal.
In his free time he enjoys working at the automatic car wash as a rotary brush.

Some of Principal Brown's favorite things:

  1. Coffee
  2. Miss Simian
  3. His hairbrush
